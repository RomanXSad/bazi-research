🔍 Focus shifts by phase

Months 1-3 — BUILD & PROBE
• Highest marketing/SEO spend relative to revenue (100% of burn)
• Dev cost dominates (building MVP + Flutter app)
• Nearly zero revenue — net loss every month
• Focus: $300 Google Ads probe, SEO content, MVP build
Months 4-6 — LAUNCH & VALIDATE
• First amulet sales start (small batch)
• Logistics + COGS appear as new cost categories
• Customer support ramps up
• Marketing spend grows (TG Ads + influencers)
• Focus: Channel validation, supply chain setup
Months 7-9 — GROWTH
• SEO compound effect — organic traffic grows
• Revenue accelerates faster than costs
• First profitable month (month 7 or 8)
• Returns/refunds become visible cost
• Focus: SEO scaling, customer retention, packaging quality
Months 10-12 — SCALE
• SEO costs flatten as % of revenue
• Logistics becomes #2 cost after COGS
• Support becomes ongoing (not project)
• Profit margin expands (marketing % drops)
• Focus: Automation, bulk shipping, subscription add-on
