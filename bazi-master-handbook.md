## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 What is BaZi?

**BaZi (八字)** literally means **"Eight Characters"** — the eight Chinese characters (4 Heavenly Stems + 4 Earthly Branches) that encode the energy of a person's birth moment. Also called **Four Pillars (四柱 / Sì Zhù)**, BaZi is a 2000+ year old system of Chinese destiny analysis rooted in the **Five Elements (五行 / Wǔ Xíng)** and **Yin-Yang (阴阳)** philosophy.

Unlike Western astrology (which uses planetary positions), BaZi analyzes the interaction of elemental forces at a specific time. It reveals innate personality, life potential, timing of opportunities, and areas of challenge.

### 1.2 The Four Pillars Concept

A BaZi chart consists of four pillars arranged as a 2×4 grid:

| | Year (年) | Month (月) | Day (日) | Hour (时) |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Heavenly Stem (天干)** | 甲/乙/... | 丙/丁/... | **戊/己/...** | 庚/辛/... |
| **Earthly Branch (地支)** | 子/丑/... | 寅/卯/... | 辰/巳/... | 午/未/... |

- **Year Pillar (年柱)** — Ancestors, family background, early life, inherited traits
- **Month Pillar (月柱)** — Parents, career direction, social life, early adulthood
- **Day Pillar (日柱)** — **Self (Day Master) and spouse** — the core of the chart
- **Hour Pillar (时柱)** — Children, late life, legacy, hidden talents

### 1.3 How It Works

1. **The Day Master (日主)** — the Heavenly Stem of the Day Pillar — represents YOU
2. **Every other character** in the chart has a **relational role** (Ten God) relative to the Day Master
3. **The Five Elements** interact through **generating (生)** and **controlling (克)** cycles
4. **The season of birth** (determined by the Month Branch) determines elemental strength
5. **Luck Pillars (大运)** and **Annual Pillars (流年)** reveal timing of life events
6. **Special patterns** override standard balance logic when one element dominates

### 1.4 Core Philosophy

> **"A BaZi chart shows potential, not predestination. Free will, environment, and effort all play crucial roles in shaping outcomes."**

#### Cycles

**Generation Cycle (相生):** Wood → Fire → Earth → Metal → Water → Wood
- Wood feeds Fire, Fire creates ash (Earth), Earth bears minerals (Metal), Metal collects Water (condensation), Water nourishes Wood

**Control Cycle (相克):** Wood → Earth → Water → Fire → Metal → Wood
- Wood breaks Earth, Earth dams Water, Water extinguishes Fire, Fire melts Metal, Metal cuts Wood

- **死** = The element controlled by 旺 (dominated to death)

### 3.1 Overview

1. **Year Pillar** — Chinese year (starts at 立春, ~Feb 4)
2. **Month Pillar** — Determined by Solar Term (节气) boundaries

4. **Hour Pillar** — Determined by time of day

### 3.3 Pillar Calculation Formulas

**Year Pillar:** 1984 = 甲子 (stem 0, branch 0). Dates before 立春 use previous year.

**Month Pillar:**
1. Determine month branch from Solar Term
2. Month stem = (Year stem index × 2 + Month offset) % 10
   - Month offset = (branch_idx - 2) % 12 (寅=0, 卯=1, ..., 丑=11)

**Day Pillar:** Reference: 1900-01-01 = 甲戌 (stem 0, branch 10). Count days from reference; stem = (ref_stem + days) % 10, branch = (ref_branch + days) % 12.

**Hour Pillar:**
1. Branch from time: 子时(23-00)=0, 丑时(01-02)=1, ..., 亥时(21-22)=11
2. Stem = (Day stem index × 2 + Hour branch index) % 10

### 3.4 Luck Pillars (大运 / Dà Yùn)

#### Direction Rule

| Condition | Direction |
|-----------|-----------|
| Yang Male (阳男) + Yin Female (阴女) | **FORWARD** (顺排) |
| Yin Male (阴男) + Yang Female (阳女) | **REVERSE** (逆排) |

#### Starting Age Calculation

Count days from birth to nearest Solar Term boundary, then convert:
- **3 days = 1 year**
- **1 day = 4 months**
- **1 two-hour period ≈ 10 days**

Forward cycle = count to **next** Solar Term. Reverse cycle = count from **previous** Solar Term.

#### Deriving Luck Pillars

Starting from the **Month Pillar**:
- **Forward:** LP(n) = Month Pillar + n stems + n branches
- **Reverse:** LP(n) = Month Pillar - n stems - n branches

Each Luck Pillar covers **10 years**. Standard: **8 Luck Pillars** (~80 years).

| LP7 | +60 to +70 | Retirement, reflection |

**Early Luck (≤6y):** Luck active from childhood. **Normal (7-13y):** Most common. **Late (≥14y):** Success comes later.

### 4.3 Element Balance Diagnosis

| Climate | Characteristics | Related Element |

### 5.2 Element Relation to Day Master

Given DM with element E:

| Relation | Element Logic | Examples (Wood DM) |
|----------|--------------|-------------------|
| Resource (印) | (E+4)%5 — produces DM | Water (壬/癸) produces Wood |

## 6. Special Patterns (特殊格局)

### 6.1 Overview: Regular vs. Special

| Dimension | Regular (正格) | Special (特殊格局) |
|-----------|---------------|-------------------|
| **Logic** | Suppress strong, support weak | Follow the strong or follow the weak |
| **DM Strength** | Moderate (score 4–7) | Overwhelmingly strong (8–10) or weak (1–3) |
| **Prevalence** | ~70–80% of charts | ~20–30% of charts |
| **Yong Shen** | Element that balances | Element that reinforces the trend |

**Always check for special patterns BEFORE applying regular balance logic.**

### 6.2 Zhuan Wang Ge (专旺格) — Special Prosperity (DM Overwhelmingly Strong)

**Conditions (ALL must be met):**
1. DM's element ≥ ~80% of chart
2. No controlling element (克) present
3. No Officer/Seven Kill in Heavenly Stems
4. Season supports DM

#### The 5 Sub-Types

| Type | Chinese | DM | Month | Forbidden |
|------|---------|----|-------|-----------|
| **Qu Ge** (Curved-Straight) | 曲直格 | Wood (木) | 寅/卯/辰 | Metal (庚/辛) |
| **Yan Shang Ge** (Blazing Flame) | 炎上格 | Fire (火) | 巳/午/未 | Water (壬/癸) |
| **Jia Se Ge** (Planting-Harvest) | 稼穑格 | Earth (土) | 辰/戌/丑/未 | Wood (甲/乙) |
| **Cong Ge** (Following-Reform) | 从革格 | Metal (金) | 申/酉/戌 | Fire (丙/丁) |
| **Run Xia Ge** (Moist-Descending) | 润下格 | Water (水) | 亥/子/丑 | Earth (戊/己) |

**Yong Shen:** DM's own element. **Ji Shen:** Element that controls DM.

### 6.3 Cong Ge (从格) — Following Patterns (DM Overwhelmingly Weak)

**Conditions (ALL must be met):**
1. DM's element ≤ ~20% of chart
2. No 印 (producer) in stems
3. No 比劫 (peers) in stems
4. Season does NOT support DM

#### The 4 Sub-Types

| Type | Chinese | Dominant | Yong Shen |
|------|---------|----------|-----------|
| **Cong Guan/Sha** (Follow Authority) | 从官/杀格 | Controls DM (克) | Controlling element |
| **Cong Cai** (Follow Wealth) | 从财格 | DM controls it (耗) | Wealth element |
| **Cong Shi Shen** (Follow Output) | 从食伤格 | Drains DM (泄) | Output element |
| **Cong Shi** (Follow Momentum) | 从势格 | Mixed opposition | Strongest opposing element |

**Yong Shen:** The dominant influencing element. **Ji Shen:** DM's own element + its producer.

### 6.4 Jia Ge (假格) — Fake / Impure Patterns

When a chart mostly qualifies as special but has minor blemishes:

| Violation | Example | Impact |
|-----------|---------|--------|
| One weak controlling element hidden | One 庚 hidden in branch | Treat as Zhuan Wang, but that element's luck years are dangerous |
| One controlling element in stem but restrained | 庚克甲 but 丙克庚 | Restrained element can't actually harm |
| Month branch slightly off | Wood DM in 辰 (Earth, but contains Water) | Borderline seasonal support |

### 6.5 Quick Diagnosis Decision Tree

```
START
  │
  ├── DM overwhelmingly STRONG (score 8-10)?
  │   │
  │   YES ──► Check for Zhuan Wang
  │   │          ├── ≥80% same element? → True Zhuan Wang

  │   │          └── Multiple supporters? → Regular (weak DM)
  │   │
  │   NO ───► REGULAR PATTERN (score 4-7)
```
