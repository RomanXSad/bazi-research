## 3. Dropshipping Options

### CJdropshipping / General Dropshipping Assessment
- **Inventory:** Many Chinese jewelry suppliers (including on Made-in-China) offer dropshipping
- **Requirements:** Typically require sales history or deposits
- **Cost structure:** Wholesale price + shipping (usually $3–$8 for small items via ePacket/SpeedPAK)
- **Platforms:** Some suppliers on CJdropshipping, Spocket, and Zendrop categories for jewelry

### Recommended Dropshipping Model for XingTu
- **Hybrid approach:** Bulk purchase + hold small inventory + ship from China directly
- **Alternative:** Use a 3PL (third-party logistics) in China for kitting + fulfillment
- **Per-unit logistics cost estimate:** $3–$6 (ePacket to US/EU, 7–15 days)
- **Faster option (7–10 days):** ~$8–$12 via expedited

## 5. Typical Lead Times

| Stage | Time |
|---|---|
| Sample production | 3–7 days |
| Bulk production (100–500 units) | 7–15 days |
| Quality control | 1–3 days |
| Shipping (ePacket/SAL to US/EU) | 7–15 days |
| Shipping (expedited DHL/FedEx) | 3–7 days |
| **Total lead time (bulk order, economy shipping)** | **14–30 days** |
| **Total lead time (dropshipping, per order)** | **10–20 days** |

**Key notes:**
- Chinese factories are fastest during Feb–June and Sep–Nov
- Avoid Chinese New Year (Jan–Feb shutdown, 2–4 week delay)
- Summer months (Jul–Aug) can have minor delays due to heat waves/power rationing
- Custom engraving adds 1–3 days to production

## 6. Existing Businesses Selling Chinese Name/Character Pendants

### Notable Competitors / Adjacent Businesses

1. **ChineseNameNecklace.com / similar Etsy shops**
   - Sells Chinese character necklaces with name meanings
   - Pricing: $25–$55
   - Style: Calligraphy on metal discs or bars

2. **OrientalFortuna / FengShuiAmulet stores**
   - Sells pre-made Chinese zodiac amulets
   - No personalization
   - Pricing: $15–$40

3. **NamePendants on Etsy**
   - Generic name pendants (English letters)
   - Very few offer Chinese character engraving
   - Gap: Chinese aesthetic + custom name combo

4. **Alibaba dropshippers (Yiwu market vendors)**
   - Hundreds of jewelry suppliers
   - Can do custom engraving in Chinese characters
   - Most require MOQ of 50–200 pieces

5. **Japanese Omamori-style amulet sellers**
   - Similar concept (embroidered pouch with charm inside)
   - Can be adapted for Chinese-style amulet
   - Pricing: $12–$25

### Market Gap Analysis
- **No existing business** combines: (a) BaZi/Astrology-generated name + (b) physical amulet fulfillment
- Chinese character pendants exist but are generic (any name, no story/meaning)
- XingTu's differentiator: The name is special — it's the user's BaZi-derived "lucky name" with a story — which justifies premium pricing ($25–$50)
- The physical amulet is a **conversation piece + souvenir** of the BaZi reading experience

## 7. Recommended Supplier Strategy

### Tier 1: Primary Supplier (Production Partner)
**Recommendation:** Use Made-in-China.com suppliers for bulk manufacturing at $2–$5/unit
- **Best match:** seami-jewelry or chenglan2014 (Chinese + English name pendants)
- **Key requirement:** Must accept custom Chinese character engraving
- **MOQ negotiation target:** 200–500 units for first batch

### Tier 2: Packaging Partner
**Recommendation:** Separate supplier for branded amulet pouches/cards
- Brocade pouch suppliers on Made-in-China: $0.30–$0.80/unit
- Custom card printing: $0.10–$0.20/unit (500+ qty)

### Tier 3: Fulfillment
- **Option A:** Ship from China (supplier handles, ~$4–$6 shipping per unit)
- **Option B:** Use a 3PL in China to receive bulk, kitting, and ship individually
- **Option C:** Dropshipping from Alibaba Express (higher per-unit cost, lower risk)

### Cost-Estimate for First Batch (500 units, basic metal with engraving + pouch)
| Item | Unit Cost | Total |
|---|---|---|
| Custom engraved pendants | $3.00 | $1,500 |
| Velvet pouches + cards | $0.60 | $300 |
| Tooling/setup (one-time) | $150 | $150 |

| **Total** | **$5.10** | **$2,700** |

### Retail Price Suggestion
- **Target retail:** $19.99–$34.99 (mid-range)
- **Gross margin:** 80–85% at COGS $5
- **After Etsy/Shopify fees (15%):** ~$12–$22 net profit per unit

## 9. Recommendations for Next Steps

1. **Contact 3–5 suppliers** from the list above with a detailed RFQ including:
   - Chinese character engraving samples (specific characters to test)
   - Custom pendant design requirements
   - MOQ for first batch (negotiate 200–500)
   - White-label/branded packaging options

2. **Request samples** from top 2–3 suppliers ($15–$50 including shipping)

3. **Test the engraving quality** — Chinese characters need precise rendering; thin strokes may not engrave well on small pendants

4. **Consider a dual-sided pendant design:** Chinese name on front, English meaning/explanation on back

5. **Evaluate packaging:** A proper "amulet experience" requires more than a simple pouch — consider a sealed amulet pouch (brocade) with meaning card inside (like Japanese omamori but Chinese-style)
