1. **Lead with "Chinese Name Generator with Meaning"** — highest volume, zero competition on mobile. The "with meaning" variant shows clear user intent for name explanation/content.

2. **Integrate BaZi as the "fortune" angle** — "BaZi reading" has strong standalone volume (avg 56.9) and is trending upward. It's the most searchable Chinese fortune method.

3. **Add Zi Wei Dou Shu calculator as premium feature** — tiny English-language competition, high ratings in Chinese ZWDS apps prove users love it. Could be a paid upgrade.
