📖 Module 1: 五行 — Five Elements

🏯 What are the Five Elements?

The Five Elements (五行, Wǔ Xíng) are Wood (木), Fire (火), Earth (土), Metal (金), and Water (水). They are the fundamental forces that interact to create all phenomena in the universe. Each element has unique qualities, colors, seasons, and emotional associations.

🧘 Personality by Element

🌳 Wood: Creative, expansive, growth-oriented. Like a tree — upward striving, flexible yet strong. Visionaries and pioneers.

🔥 Fire: Passionate, charismatic, expressive. Like a flame — warm, illuminating, but can burn out. Leaders and performers.

⛰️ Earth: Stable, nurturing, practical. Like soil — grounded, reliable, abundant. Healers and caretakers.

⚙️ Metal: Structured, disciplined, decisive. Like a blade — sharp, clear, strong. Judges and organizers.

💧 Water: Wise, adaptable, introspective. Like water — fluid, deep, persistent. Philosophers and strategists.

☀️ Module 2: 天干 — Ten Heavenly Stems

🏮 What are the Heavenly Stems?

The Ten Heavenly Stems (天干, Tiān Gān) are the celestial counterpart to the earthly branches. They represent the yang and yin forms of each of the Five Elements. Each stem has a polarity (yang/yin), an element, and a directional association.

🔗 Stem Combinations (天干合)

Certain stems combine when they meet, transforming into a different element. These are called 合化 (hé huà) combinations:

StemsCombined Element
甲 (Jiǎ) + 己 (Jǐ)Earth 土
乙 (Yǐ) + 庚 (Gēng)Metal 金
丙 (Bǐng) + 辛 (Xīn)Water 水
丁 (Dīng) + 壬 (Rén)Wood 木
戊 (Wù) + 癸 (Guǐ)Fire 火

💡 Tip: These are always Yang+Yin pairs of adjacent elements in the generation cycle.

🐉 Module 3: 地支 — Twelve Earthly Branches

🐲 What are the Earthly Branches?

The Twelve Earthly Branches (地支, Dì Zhī) correspond to the 12 animals of the Chinese zodiac. Each branch has an associated element, polarity, and season. They govern the earthly energies in a BaZi chart.

🤝 Branch Interactions

六合 (Six Combinations — 六合):

子丑合 (Rat+Ox) → Earth · 寅亥合 (Tiger+Pig) → Wood · 卯戌合 (Rabbit+Dog) → Fire
辰酉合 (Dragon+Rooster) → Metal · 巳申合 (Snake+Monkey) → Water · 午未合 (Horse+Goat) → Sun/Heat

⚔️ 六冲 (Six Clashes):

子午冲 (Rat vs Horse) · 丑未冲 (Ox vs Goat) · 寅申冲 (Tiger vs Monkey)
卯酉冲 (Rabbit vs Rooster) · 辰戌冲 (Dragon vs Dog) · 巳亥冲 (Snake vs Pig)

三合 (Three Harmonies — 三合局):

申子辰 (Monkey+Rat+Dragon) → Water · 亥卯未 (Pig+Rabbit+Goat) → Wood
寅午戌 (Tiger+Horse+Dog) → Fire · 巳酉丑 (Snake+Rooster+Ox) → Metal

🗂️ What are Hidden Stems?

Each Earthly Branch contains Hidden Stems (藏干, Cáng Gān) — Heavenly Stems that reside inside the branch. These inner energies influence the branch's expression and are crucial for detailed BaZi analysis.

👥 What are the Ten Gods?

The Ten Gods (十神, Shí Shén) describe the relationships between each Heavenly Stem in your chart and your Day Master (日主, the stem of your birth day). They reveal your relationships, career, wealth, and inner self.
