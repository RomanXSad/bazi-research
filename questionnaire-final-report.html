Critical finding: At Moderate scenario (15k visitors/mo), net profit is only $476/mo — not a business, a side project. At Optimistic scenario (50k visitors/mo), break-even is 6.3 months with $57k/yr profit. The key lever is traffic.
Key unit economics (blended) CALCULATED
Blended ASP: $25.00 (40% Budget + 40% Mid + 20% Premium)
Blended COGS: $5.80 per unit
Stripe fees: ~$1.03 per order
Gross margin after fees: $18.18 per order (72.7%)
Zero LLM costs (local engine) — huge advantage over AI SaaS model
Marketing impact WARNING
At Moderate scale, $500/mo marketing wipes out all profit (net goes negative).
Marketing only makes sense at Optimistic traffic levels (50k+ visitors/mo).
Conclusion: viral/organic growth is NOT optional — it's the only viable path until hitting scale.
