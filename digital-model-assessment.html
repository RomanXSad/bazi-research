⚠️ White spots — what we DON'T know (and need to validate)

🔴 SPOT 1: Will people actually pay $9.99 for a BaZi name report PDF?

This is the #1 risk. Co-Star (most comparable) has only 0.25-1% free→paid conversion. Etsy BaZi PDFs sell for $3-15 but data is anecdotal. We don't know if "Chinese name" has enough purchase intent. Every competitor gives the chart away for free. The paid product layer is a hypothesis, not a fact.

🟠 SPOT 2: What's the actual free→paid conversion for BaZi specifically?

Our model assumes 4-6% report conversion. That's based on general astrology benchmarks. But:
• BaZi is less known than Western astrology → likely lower awareness
• "Chinese name generator" searchers may want free novelty, not a paid service
• Estimated 2-8% range is wide — $3k vs $48k/mo difference

🟠 SPOT 3: Can daily fortune subscription sustain under 12% monthly churn?

Astrology apps see 8-20% monthly churn. Co-Star: 8-15%. 40-60% of new subs cancel in first month. If churn is 15-20%, subscriber economics don't work (2-3 months average lifetime vs 12-month payback). Need a pilot to measure actual subscription retention for daily BaZi.

🟡 SPOT 4: Can we rank for "BaZi name report" or "Chinese name generator" SEO?

"Chinese name generator" needs 50-150 referring domains, 6-12 months, established players (YourChineseAstrology.com DR 65-75). "BaZi name report" is lower difficulty (20-50 domains, 3-6 months) but has only ~1k-3k searches/mo. The SEO playbook exists but the timeline is long and content cost is real ($50-100/article).

🟡 SPOT 5: What's the actual CPC on "Chinese name generator" Google Ads?

The $300 Google Ads probe test is still the most important action. We hypothesize CPC under $1.50. If CPC is actually $3-5 because of low search volume, the whole model changes (paid acquisition becomes too expensive even for digital). This single data point validates or invalidates the entire traffic thesis.

⚪ SPOT 6: Upsell conversion — will report buyers upgrade to subscription?

Assumed 5-15% of one-time buyers convert to subscription. No data for BaZi specifically.

⚪ SPOT 7: Daily fortune — does anyone care about daily BaZi?

Western daily horoscopes work (Co-Star, The Pattern). But BaZi is cycle-based (10-year luck pillars), not daily. "Daily BaZi fortune" may be an unnatural product — users may not come back daily. Need behavioral testing.

⚪ SPOT 8: TikTok viral potential for "Chinese name" content?

Viral strategy document exists (12 content themes, week-1 calendar). But the actual viral coefficient is unknown. One hit video = 100k visits. Zero hits = $500/mo in Google Ads traffic. High variance.

📝 Final assessment

Digital-only IS a better business than amulets. This is clear. 90-97% margin vs 38%. Zero returns. Zero inventory. Zero shipping. $9.99 report economics work at much lower traffic levels ($800/mo at just 10k visits conservative).
But the core uncertainty is not "product" — it's "traffic + conversion". The $300 Google Ads probe test is the single highest-leverage action you can take. If CPC < $1.50 and completion > 50%, build the digital product. If CPC > $3 or completion < 30%, pivot — not to another product, but to a different acquisition channel entirely.
Best case scenario: Build the free name generator as an SEO asset → 50k visits/mo organic → 6% report conversion → $19.5k/mo reports + $3.7k/mo subscriptions + $5.8k/mo upsells = $29k/mo revenue, $26k/mo profit, break-even on $30k seed in 2 months.
Worst case scenario: CPC > $3, completion < 30%, no viral content traction → $800 probe shows the project doesn't work → total sunk cost $1,100 ($300 probe + $500 landing page + $300 content). Better to know this in 30 days than after building an MVP for 3 months.
