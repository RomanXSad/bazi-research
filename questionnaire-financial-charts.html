Assumptions behind ranges:
• Y1 Low: 5k–10k visits/mo avg, 20–50 orders/mo, $0–300/mo marketing | Y1 High: 25k–40k visits/mo avg (SEO ramps), 100–200 orders/mo, $500/mo avg marketing
• Y2 Low: Traffic plateaus at 20k/mo, 80–100 orders/mo | Y2 High: Traffic reaches 50k–80k/mo (SEO mature), 200–400 orders/mo
• COGS uses blended 40/40/20 mix (Budget/Mid/Premium) at $5.80 avg
• Returns calculated at 4% of revenue (physical goods norm)
• Contingency is 10% of revenue to cover unexpected costs (chargebacks, fraud, shipping damage, platform fee changes)
• App maintenance assumes solo developer ($50/hr equivalent, ~4-10 hrs/mo)
• Does NOT include founder salary — this is side-project economics
